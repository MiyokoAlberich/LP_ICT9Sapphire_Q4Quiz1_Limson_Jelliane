Mark Edward Fischbach



Mark Edward Fischbach (born June 28, 1989), known online as Markiplier, is an American YouTuber, actor and filmmaker. Mark Edward Fischbach was born on June 28, 1989, in Honolulu, Hawaii. His father Cliffton was a German American military officer who met his Korean mother Sunok Frank while stationed in South Korea. Fischbach's maternal family history is explored in the 2022 documentary Markiplier from North Korea.

Fischbach is one of the most popular gaming YouTubers on the platform. He was listed by Forbes as the third highest-paid content creator on the platform in 2022, and has won four Streamy Awards and a Golden Joystick Award for "Best Streamer/Broadcaster".
